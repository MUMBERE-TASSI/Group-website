About the Cybersecurity Club

The Cybersecurity Club is dedicated to equipping students with the skills and knowledge to protect digital systems, networks, and data from threats. We focus on ethical hacking, penetration testing, and cybersecurity awareness.

Our Objectives

  • Learn ethical hacking tools and techniques.
  • Understand network security fundamentals.
  • Participate in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions.
  • Raise cybersecurity awareness within the JKUAT community.
  • Collaborate on real-world security projects.

Activities & Workshops

  • Weekly hacking labs and challenges.
  • Cyber defense simulations.
  • Guest talks from industry professionals.
  • Bug bounty programs and responsible disclosure practice.

Join Us

Whether you're an aspiring ethical hacker or just curious about online safety, the Cybersecurity Club welcomes all skill levels.
