Be a Force for Good

The JKUAT Red Cross Chapter is a student-led arm of the Kenya Red Cross Society (KRCS), dedicated to humanitarian service, disaster response, and community empowerment both on and off campus.

Overview

The chapter serves as a bridge between students and the humanitarian mission of the Red Cross. We mobilize student volunteers to respond to emergencies, support community health initiatives, and promote humanitarian values.

  • Emergency Preparedness
  • First Aid Training
  • Disaster Response
  • Community Outreach

History

Established in [YEAR], the JKUAT Red Cross Chapter joined the network of campus chapters under KRCS. Over the years, we have grown into an active unit recognized for quick mobilization and impactful humanitarian projects.

Main Activities

  • First Aid drills & certification programs
  • Blood donation drives
  • Community clean-ups
  • Campus health awareness campaigns
  • Disaster risk reduction workshops

Membership

All JKUAT students are welcome to join. Membership offers:

  • Official KRCS membership card
  • Access to certified training
  • Opportunities to lead projects
  • Networking with humanitarian workers

How to Volunteer

  1. Register with the chapter during recruitment drives or via our online form.
  2. Attend orientation and induction training.
  3. Participate in scheduled activities and events.
  4. Stay active to qualify for advanced training opportunities.
