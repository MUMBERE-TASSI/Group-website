CHESS CLUB

The JKUAT Chess Club is a hub for strategic thinkers, passionate players, and those curious about the royal game. Whether you're a beginner or a grandmaster in the making, this is your space to grow, learn, and compete.

Our Goals:

  • Promote critical thinking and problem-solving through chess.
  • Organize internal tournaments and represent JKUAT in national events.
  • Create a welcoming environment for students of all skill levels.
